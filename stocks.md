> :warning: These tests are informative only, and do not promise any actual returns.
The ranking is done using current data, but the tests are backward looking.
Survivorship and selection biases will make backtesting look better than what will happen going forward.
For example, most momentum stocks performed well recently, so automatically the performance of the backtest looks better.
